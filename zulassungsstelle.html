The process

How it works

Five steps from booking to driving away with new plates.

  1. 01
    Book your appointment online
    Use service.bremen.de to choose a date and time. Almost all services require an appointment — walk-ins are accepted only for limited enquiries.
  2. 02
    Gather your documents
    Use the checklist below. Bring originals — copies aren't accepted at the counter. For unusual cases (imports, company registrations) call ahead.
  3. 03
    Arrive on time at Stresemannstraße 48
    Bring your appointment confirmation (digital or printed). Check in at the entrance terminal and wait for your number to be called.
  4. 04
    Submit at the counter
    A clerk reviews your documents, takes payment (EC card or cash — no credit cards), and processes the registration in the KBA system.
  5. 05
    Collect your documents and plates
    Receive your Zulassungsbescheinigung Teil I and stamped plates. Total time on-site with an appointment: 30–60 minutes.

FAQ

Bürgeramt Bremen — common questions

Do I need an appointment?+
Yes — almost all services require booking online via service.bremen.de. Walk-ins are accepted only for very few enquiries.
How long is the typical wait?+
With a booked appointment, processing takes 30–60 minutes once you arrive. Without an appointment, expect 2–4 hours during peak periods.
Can someone else register the car for me?+
Yes — with a signed Vollmacht (power of attorney) and a copy of your ID, a representative can complete the registration.
What are the official fees?+
Standard new registration is around €30 at the Zulassungsstelle, plus plate-stamping fees. Total cost typically lands between €70–€90 including plates.
