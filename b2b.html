What does it cost to join?+
There's no upfront fee or monthly subscription. You pay €5 per registration processed through the platform, plus the pass-through ZulassungsbehÃ¶rde fees which you charge to the end customer.
Do I need special hardware or software?+
No. Everything runs in the browser. You'll need a card reader for eID verification (which we can supply at cost) and a printer for plate confirmation documents. No installs, no servers.
How many registrations can I realistically process per day?+
With pre-uploaded customer documents and our KBA integration, most partners handle 8–15 registrations per workstation per day. The average per-case time is around 14 minutes.
What happens if a customer arrives without documents?+
The platform requires customers to upload required documents (eVB, Personalausweis, Fahrzeugschein) before booking. Incomplete bookings don't reach you — you only see verified cases.
Can I use this alongside walk-ins?+
Yes. The platform is purely additive. Many of our partners use it exclusively for online-booked cases and keep their existing walk-in workflow unchanged.
What support do you provide?+
Onboarding includes a walkthrough, training materials, and a named contact for your first 30 days. Ongoing support is via in-app chat and a dedicated partner success team during business hours.

How long does it take to go live?+
Once you complete the onboarding call, your team can process registrations the same day. The web dashboard requires no installation — just a browser login. API integrations typically take 1–2 weeks depending on your DMS.
Which DMS systems do you integrate with?+
We support integration via REST API with any system that can make HTTP requests. Pre-built connectors for common German DMS platforms are in development. Contact us with your specific system and we'll let you know the current status.
Are KBA authority fees included in the €15?+
No. ZulassungsbehÃ¶rde fees (approx. €26–€35 per case depending on district) are passed through at cost and invoiced separately. The €15 covers our platform and KBA API connection.
How does eID verification work for our customers?+
When a registration is initiated, the customer receives a secure link via SMS or email. They verify their identity using their Personalausweis eID and sign the digital Vollmacht on their phone via AusweisApp2. No paper required, legally binding throughout.

How do I get sandbox access?+
Sign up via the form and you'll receive sandbox credentials within 5 minutes. No agreement needed for sandbox — only for production traffic.
What's the typical integration timeline?+
For API integration, most teams ship a working flow in 1–2 weeks. White-label shop deployment takes 48 hours from signed agreement, since we handle the build.
How is eID handled in an API context?+
We provide a hosted identity flow you can redirect users to, or embed via iframe. The user completes eID verification on their phone and we return them to your platform with a signed authorization token.
What happens if the KBA system is unavailable?+
We queue requests and retry automatically with exponential back-off. Your webhook receives the final status either way. KBA system status is exposed via a /v1/health endpoint for your own monitoring.
