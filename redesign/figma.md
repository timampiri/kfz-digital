## Logo Concept Designs

### **1. Network Points**
- **Title**: "Option 1: Network Points"
- **Visual**: 
  - Center circle (Ø 35px, green #2D9D78)
  - 5 surrounding circles (Ø 24px) at compass points
- **Description**: "Franchise connectivity • Scalable"

### **2. Checkmark + Speed**
- **Title**: "Option 2: Checkmark + Speed"
- **Visual**:
  - Dynamic checkmark (2 strokes, 9px weight, rounded caps)
  - Left part: 45° angle, 35×35px
  - Right part: 50×-70px (diagonal down-right)
- **Description**: "Efficiency & completion • Bold"

### **3. License Plate Frame**
- **Title**: "Option 3: License Plate"
- **Visual**:
  - Rectangle 105×80px, green outline (3px), white fill, slight corner radius
  - 2 horizontal dividing lines inside
  - Checkmark (✓) symbol in top-left area, green
- **Description**: "Product reference • Distinctive"

### **4. Location Pin + Car**
- **Title**: "Option 4: Pin + Car"
- **Visual**:
  - Large circle (Ø 62px, blue #0066CC) as pin background
  - Car silhouette inside (white):
    - Body: 25×13px rectangle
    - 2 wheels: 8×8px circles below body
- **Description**: "Find + register • Intuitive"

### **5. Platform Hub**
- **Title**: "Option 5: Platform Hub"
- **Visual**:
  - Central circle (Ø 25px, green)
