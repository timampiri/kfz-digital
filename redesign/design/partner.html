What we do

Registration services

Every i-Kfz process — handled in person at our Bremen office or by document pickup.

Neuzulassung

Most popular

First-time registration for new or previously unregistered vehicles. We handle all KBA paperwork digitally.

Umschreibung / Halterwechsel

Re-registration after buying a used vehicle. Ownership transfer handled completely on your behalf.

Abmeldung

Same day

Deregister your vehicle quickly. Deregistration certificate issued on the same day in most cases.

Wiederzulassung

Re-register a previously deregistered vehicle. All documentation prepared and submitted for you.

Adress- / Namensänderung

Update your registration details after moving or a name change (e.g. marriage).

Technische Änderung / Eintragung

Technical modifications entered into the registration certificate — tuning, conversions, and more.

Current processing times: Neuzulassung, Umschreibung, and Wiederzulassung currently take 1–3 business days due to Zulassungsstelle workload. Abmeldung is still processed same-day.
Prices vary by service, Bundesland authority fees, and current plate / document costs. You'll see the exact total in the first step of the booking flow — no surprises after.
The process

How it works

Five simple steps from your doorstep to fresh plates.

  1. 01
    Prepare your documents
    Use the checklist below to gather everything you need. If you're unsure, call us — we'll confirm the exact list for your case before you come in.
  2. 02
    Visit our office — no appointment needed
    Walk in Mon–Fri 07:00–17:00 at Falkenweg 16, 28865 Lilienthal. Hand over your documents. We check everything on the spot and confirm we have what we need.
  3. 03
    We handle the Zulassungsstelle
    We submit your application to the KFZ-Zulassungsbehörde Bremen via direct KBA connection — digitally, with priority access. You don't need to visit the authority yourself.
  4. 04
    We notify you when it's done
    You'll receive a call or SMS when your documents and plates are ready to collect. Most registrations completed within 24–48 hours.
  5. 05
    Collect your documents and plates
    Pick up your Zulassungsbescheinigung Teil I, updated documents, and fresh plates from our office. Drive away the same day you collect.
Can't make it to our office? We also offer document pickup and delivery by courier within Bremen. Contact us to arrange.

Help

Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers about documents, timing, and what we can handle on your behalf.

Do I need an appointment?+
No appointment needed. Walk in Mon–Fri 07:00–17:00. For large volumes or complex cases, calling ahead is welcome but not required.
Can you register a car that was first registered before 2015?+
Yes — this is one of our main advantages over purely online services. We have direct KBA access and can handle vehicles registered before 2015, which are not eligible for the i-Kfz self-service portal.
Can you handle registrations for companies and dealerships?+
Yes. We serve private customers, commercial businesses, car dealerships, and fleet operators. For dealerships with high volumes, contact us for a custom pricing agreement.
What if my documents have an error or discrepancy?+
We review all documents when you drop them off. If we spot an issue, we'll tell you immediately — before submitting anything — so you can resolve it without unnecessary delays.
Can you register an imported or foreign vehicle?+
Yes, but requirements vary significantly depending on the country of origin and vehicle type. Please call us before bringing in an imported vehicle so we can confirm exactly what documentation you'll need.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept EC card (Girocard), cash, and bank transfer. Credit cards are not accepted for authority fee payments — please bring EC card or cash for the Zulassungsbehörde portion.
