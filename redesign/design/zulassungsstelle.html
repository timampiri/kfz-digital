OFFICIAL REGISTRATION OFFICE

Bürgeramt Bremen — KFZ-Zulassung

The official vehicle registration office for the Hansestadt Bremen. Appointments required for most services. Use the contact details below or skip the queue with a KFZ Digital partner.

SERVICES

What you can do here

  • Vehicle registration (Zulassung)
  • Vehicle deregistration online
  • Owner enquiry (Halterauskunft)
  • Short-term plates (Kurzzeitkennzeichen)
  • Environment badge (Umweltplakette)
  • Address change — within Bremen
  • Address change — moving in
  • Lost plates (Verlust Kennzeichen)
  • Lost registration certificate I
  • Lost registration certificate II
DOCUMENTS

What to bring for a new registration

  • 01 Personalausweis or passport with confirmation of address
  • 02 Fahrzeugbrief (Zulassungsbescheinigung Teil II)
  • 03 Fahrzeugschein (Zulassungsbescheinigung Teil I), for used vehicles
  • 04 eVB-Nummer from your insurer
  • 05 SEPA-Mandat for vehicle tax direct debit
  • 06 Valid HU/AU report (TÜV), for used vehicles

FAQ

Bürgeramt Bremen — common questions

Do I need an appointment?+
Yes — almost all services require booking online via service.bremen.de. Walk-ins are accepted only for very few enquiries.
How long is the typical wait?+
With a booked appointment, processing takes 30–60 minutes once you arrive. Without an appointment, expect 2–4 hours during peak periods.
Can someone else register the car for me?+
Yes — with a signed Vollmacht (power of attorney) and a copy of your ID, a representative can complete the registration.
What are the official fees?+
Standard new registration is around €30 at the Zulassungsstelle, plus plate-stamping fees. Total cost typically lands between €70–€90 including plates.
