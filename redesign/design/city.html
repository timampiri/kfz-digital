FAQ

How to register your car fast in Bremen

How long does it take at a Bremen partner?+
Around 15 minutes from check-in to printed plates. No appointment needed at any of the three partner points.
What documents do I need?+
Personalausweis, Fahrzeugbrief (ZB II), Fahrzeugschein (ZB I), eVB-Nummer from your insurer, and SEPA-Mandat for vehicle tax.
Do I need an appointment to register fast in Bremen?+
No. All three Bremen partner points are walk-in — no booking, no waiting weeks for a Zulassungsstelle slot. Bring your documents and you'll leave with printed HB-plates the same day.
Do I still need to visit the Zulassungsstelle?+
No. Our partners handle the entire registration with the Zulassungsstelle on your behalf — including paying fees and issuing plates.

FAQ

How to register your car fast in Aalen

What's the fastest way to register an AA-plated car?+
Order online via our partner KennzeichenMax. AA-plates arrive by DHL within 1–3 working days, and the digital submission to the Zulassungsstelle is usually processed the same day — no in-person trip needed.
Can I speed it up if I'm in a hurry?+
Reserve your Wunschkennzeichen online first so the submission isn't held up at the plate-allocation step. If you can't wait for delivery, book an online appointment with the Aalen Zulassungsstelle directly and bring your documents in person.
What documents speed things up the most?+
Have these ready before you start: Personalausweis (with eID activated), Fahrzeugbrief (ZB II), Fahrzeugschein (ZB I), eVB-Nummer from your insurer, and SEPA-Mandat for vehicle tax. With everything prepared, the online submission takes about 10 minutes.
