FAQ

Common questions, answered.

What do I need for online registration?

Typically: your Zulassungsbescheinigung Teil I & II, a valid ID (Personalausweis or passport), an eVB number from your insurance, and a current TÜV/HU certificate. We guide you through the exact list for your case during the application.

How does the identity verification work?

Two options: the German ID card (Personalausweis) with eID activated — quickest, ~2 minutes via the AusweisApp; or video identification with a trained agent. Both are legally compliant under federal i-Kfz standards.

When will I receive my plates?

Provisional registration arrives by email instantly upon completion. Physical plates and stickers are delivered by DHL within 2–4 working days, or you can collect them same-day at a partner point.

What does registration really cost?

Online: €99.90 all-in (our service fee plus all official Behördengebühren). Partner point: from €49.90 plus official fees. Wunschkennzeichen reservation: €19.90, combinable with registration. No hidden charges — what you see at checkout is the final amount.

What happens if online registration doesn't work?

If we can't complete the digital process despite correct data on your side, you get a full refund — no questions asked. We'll also help route you to your nearest partner point or registration office.

Is digital registration legally valid?

Yes — fully. Digital registration is regulated under the German Fahrzeug-Zulassungsverordnung (FZV) and the i-Kfz framework operated by the Kraftfahrt-Bundesamt. We are a recognised partner connected to the official i-Kfz interface.
