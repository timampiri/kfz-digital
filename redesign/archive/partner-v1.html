The process

How it works

01

Prepare your documents

Use the checklist below to gather everything you need. If you're unsure, call us — we'll confirm the exact list for your case before you come in.

02

Visit our office — no appointment needed

Walk in Mon–Fri 07:00–17:00 at Falkenweg 16, 28865 Lilienthal. Hand over your documents. We check everything on the spot and confirm we have what we need.

03

We handle the Zulassungsstelle

We submit your application to the KFZ-Zulassungsbehörde Bremen via direct KBA connection — digitally, with priority access. You don't need to visit the authority yourself.

04

We notify you when it's done

You'll receive a call or SMS when your documents and plates are ready to collect. Most registrations completed within 24–48 hours.

05

Collect your documents and plates

Pick up your Zulassungsbescheinigung Teil I, updated documents, and fresh plates from our office. Drive away the same day you collect.

Can't make it to our office? We also offer document pickup and delivery by courier within Bremen. Contact us to arrange.
