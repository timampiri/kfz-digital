# KFZ Digital Platform — Business Context

Reference for the long-term platform vision. The current landing page redesign (b2c.html, b2b.html, partner.html) targets Phase 1 customer acquisition.

---

## Overview

KFZ Digital targets ~30,000 German car dealerships with a platform to digitize operations. B2B core value: dealerships register free → upsell ecosystem (marketplace, widgets, site builder).

---

## Business Model

### Phase 1 (Current): Registration Platform
- Free registration service for dealerships
- White-label branding (partners use own domain/logo)
- AI-powered customer management agents

### Phase 2: Marketplace & Monetization
- Marketplace (fixtures, accessories, supplies)
- Communication widgets (customer + team chat)
- Token-based premium features
- Website builder for dealerships without sites

---

## Target Market

- 30,000 car dealerships in Germany
- SMB segments with minimal tech infrastructure
- Initial focus: 50+ car dealerships, no digital strategy

---

## Key Features (Platform)

1. **Registration Flow** — 10-minute online dealership signup; government integration; mobile-first; white-label pages per partner
2. **AI Agent System** — Marketing (upsell analysis), Operational (email automation, orders), Accounting (invoicing, tracking)
3. **Partner Integration** — KFZ Digital MAX (clone into React), Mobegin (mobility platform)
